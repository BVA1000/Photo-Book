This was taken somewhere between Trinidad and Crescent City, California. I took a trip with some of my friends to visit my parents for the weekend who live up in Brookings, Oregon. It was a beautiful short trip. This part of the West Coast in Northern California and Southern Oregon was peaceful and breathtaking for me.
