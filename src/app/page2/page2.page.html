Page 2 Page 2 Captiva Island A place I really love
Captiva Island is a beautiful island located on the west gulf coast of Florida about 40 minutes away from Fort Myers. It was a family vacation which took place last year in 2020 where we spent a week enjoying the beach, boating, ocean fishing, and is a great place for sea shell hunting. We were celebrating my grandma's 75th and grandpa's 70th Birthdays as well as seeing what it was like for our parents who also explored the area 35 years ago.
